 java_built_in_exceptions
 ========================
 
 Subclasses of the "RuntimeException" class are unchecked exceptions
 and need not be included in any method's throws lists.
 Other exceptions are checked exceptions and they need be included in 
 method's throws lists.

 Practically all java system exceptions are contained in the "java.lang" package.
